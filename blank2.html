The science behind the safety.

What is magetism? Force that caused by magnets that causes repel or attract each other. (https://byjus.com/physics/magnetic-force/)

Magnetic force: the force arises due to interacting magnetic fields (https://byjus.com/physics/magnetic-force/)

Magnetic poles: region at the two end of magnet, the area of the strongest magnetic field. (https://www.britannica.com/science/magnetic-pole)

Magnetic field: vector quantities that exist in a region in space where a magnetic force occurs.

Magnetic flux: The number of magnetic field lines passing through a surface (textbook pg 646)

Domain: When the magnetic fields of the electrons in a group of neighboring atoms are all aligned in the same direction (textbook pg 650)

Electromagnetism

Differentiate between permanent and temporary magnets

Permanent magnets: The magnetism of permanent magnets is produced in the same way in which you created the magnetism of the nail. Because of the microscopic structure of the magnet material, the induced magnetism becomes permanent. Many permanent magnets are made of an iron alloy called ALNICO V, that contains a mix of aluminum, nickel, and cobalt. A variety of rare earth elements, such as neodymium and gadolinium, produce permanent magnets that are extremely strong for their size. (textbook page 645)

Temporary magnets: The magnets that were polarized by the permanent magnets, if one pull away the permanent magnet, temporary loses some of its attraction (textbook pg 645)

Describe the relationship between electricity and magnetism:

The direction of the proton moving through the wire produces magnetic field. The direction of magnetic field is determined by the direction of electric current. These action together is called electromagnetism. (https://www.physics-and-radio-electronics.com/physics/magnetism/relation-between-electricity-and-magnetism.html)
