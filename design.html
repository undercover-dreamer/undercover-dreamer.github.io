the secret to the safety

Built to last. Built to save.



Dimensions. It’ll blow your mind.

WIth a standard sized tesla coming in at 16 feet. The product reaches over 50 feet to adequately cover the perimeter of the vehicle.

Appearance. See for yourself.

During installation safemag will be placed underneath the lining of the car, so it won't be seen from the exterior of the vehicle.

Durability. Unmatched.

Safemag owners can depend on the added security while not suffering from any modifications to the sleek design of the vehicle.

Product Material. Purposeful.

Safemag is made out of electromagnets that adhere to the vehicle magnetically. Uses no materials other than high strength electromagnets and a few other trade secrets.

Strength. Checkmate.

Safemags strength is unmatched in the car safety arena. Due to its durable design that allows for it to repell other vehicles up to 10,000 lbs, traveling up to 120mph. Safety is not compromised for the everyday thrill seeker.
