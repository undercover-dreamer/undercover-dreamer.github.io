Product Purpose

_________________________________

truly multipurpose!

Purpose One. Repelling security.

When the sensors previously installed into the vehicle sense another vehicle is approaching at over 10 miles per hour in speed, the electromagnets are activated and repel the oncoming vehicle with its own magnetic field.

Purpose Two. External storage.

When activated by the driver in the internal control panel of the vehicle, a specific electromagnet is enabled which allows for others items such as bicycles or storage boxes to be securly attached to the vehicle until it is deactivated.
